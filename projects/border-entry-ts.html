US Mexico Border Time Series Analysis

← Back to Portfolio

Overview

Analyzed monthly U.S.-Mexico border entry data spanning 28 years using the Box-Jenkins approach to identify trends and forecast future entries. An SARIMA model achieving the lowest AIC was chosen to model the time series. Applied spectral analysis to detect seasonal patterns and improve model performance, demonstrating the ability to enhance forecasting precision for long-term planning.
